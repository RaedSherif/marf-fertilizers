Step-by-Step Guide

How to apply marf

Simple and easy to use. Just follow these 4 steps and your crops will feel the difference from the very first application.

1
💧

Dilute with Water

Mix marf with clean water according to the recommended ratio on the bottle label.

2
🌅

Apply Early Morning

Apply during early morning or late evening to avoid midday heat. This ensures maximum absorption.

3
🪣

Spray or Pour

Use a sprayer for foliar application on leaves, or pour gently around the base of the plant for root feeding.

4
📅

Repeat Every 2–3 Weeks

Apply regularly throughout the growing season for best results. Consistent use means stronger, healthier crops.

📋

Important Tips

  • → Do not apply in direct midday sun — it reduces effectiveness.
  • → Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • → Shake the bottle well before each use to mix the formula.
  • → For best results, water your crops lightly after application.
